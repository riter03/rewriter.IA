What is a rewrite tool?

Paraphrasing tool is a program that makes it possible to rewrite a text so that words and sentences differ from the original, but the original meaning remains the same.

Do rewrite tools work?

Yes, it works, you change whole sentences and get a copy of the text in the sentence, but with different words and sentences, making your text more unique.

Is the paraphrasing tool free?

Not all such tools are free. For example, NeuralWriter is a free tool, but works better than the paid versions.

Is paraphrasing illegal?

This method is completely legal. You are not stealing the exact text from the original, you are just retelling the meaning in different words.

Do paraphrasing tools count as plagiarism?

Some tools simply change the words into synonyms, making it plagiarism. For example, our free tool NeuralWriter changes entire phrases, making it less likely to be detected as plagiarism.
